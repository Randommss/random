FACULTIES

FACULTY OF MANAGEMENT STUDIES

The Faculty of Management Studies was established in 1997 under the Pokhara University Act. Pokhara University has offered Bachelor, Master and PhD programs to prospective students under the Faculty of Management Studies.

FACULTY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

The Faculty of Science and Technology was established in 1997 under the Pokhara University Act. Pokhara University has offered Bachelor and Master programs to prospective students under the Faculty of Science and Technology.

FACULTY OF HEALTH SCIENCES

The Faculty of Health Sciences (FHS) was established in 2016 under the Pokhara University Act. Pokhara University has offered Bachelor and Master programs to prospective students under the Faculty of Health Sciences.

FACULTY OF HUMANITIES AND SOCIAL STUDIES

The Faculty of Humanities & Social Science was established in 2004 under the Pokhara University Act. Pokhara University has offered Bachelor, Master, MPhil and PhD programs to prospective students under the Faculty of Humanities & Social Sciences.
